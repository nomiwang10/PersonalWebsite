About Me

Hello! I'm Nomi, an electrical engineering student who likes to apply what I learned in school to create and engineer cool stuff. My journey into tech started years ago with a fascination when I was learning electromagnetic theory back in high school when I was in Shanghai, China.

Later on when I was applying to college I found out Electrical Engineering is the major which I can combine what I am good at(Math & Physics) with something new I want to learn(hardware design and coding).

I'm interested in embedded systems, hardware design, IoT, and control systems, enjoying the challenge of building a project from the ground up. I thrive on bringing ideas to life and am always eager to learn new technologies and improve my craft.

In my free time, you can find me going to a gym and working out, brewing the perfect cup of coffee, or diving into a good book.
